Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs based on usage patterns.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secured database accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords.

You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly currently does not share your personal data with third-party services. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. This is a client-side request from your browser directly to Google and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
